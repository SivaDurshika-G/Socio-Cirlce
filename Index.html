🌍 SocioCircle

"Where Surplus Meets Need"

🔹 Problem

Every day, tons of food and usable items are wasted, while millions struggle without basic needs. 👉 There is no structured platform that connects donors and NGOs in real-time for smooth pickups and distribution.

🔹 Solution

SocioCircle bridges the gap between donors, NGOs, and volunteers. ✅ Donors can post surplus food, clothes, books ✅ NGOs can claim pickups & deliver them ✅ Live tracking like Swiggy/Zomato ✅ Dashboard shows your impact
